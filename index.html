The solution at a glance

RailAI orchestrates precise train movements across constrained sections by forecasting conflicts, optimizing headways, and issuing real‑time control advisories to maintain stable flow and maximize capacity.

Predict

Short‑term forecasts of train arrival times, dwell, and clearance buffers.

Optimize

Solve rolling‑horizon schedules to maintain safe, minimal headways under variability.

Control

Generate speed, dwell, and sequence advisories integrated with existing signaling.

How it works

  1. 1

    Data fusion

    Telemetry, signaling, and timetable context drive probabilistic arrival predictions.

  2. 2

    Rolling optimization

    Every few seconds, a safe sequence and target times are recomputed to stabilize headways.

  3. 3

    Closed‑loop control

    Advisories are sent to drivers or ATO to adjust speed and dwell for smooth flow.

Safety‑first constraints

  • Minimum signal spacing and braking curves respected at all times
  • Platform dwell and clearance buffers enforced
  • Conflict resolution with priorities and knock‑on delay minimization

FAQ

Does this replace existing signaling?

No. RailAI operates above your existing signaling, issuing advisory controls while respecting all safety constraints.

What is required to get started?

Telemetry feeds, historic timetable data, and a section model. A pilot can be deployed in weeks.

How do you quantify benefits?

We run before/after A/B trials on selected sections, tracking throughput, delay minutes, and on‑time arrival.
